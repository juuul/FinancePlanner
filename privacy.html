Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

Introduction

Finance Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you visit our website financeplannerapp.com (the "Website") or use our mobile applications (the "App").

The most important thing to know: All your personal financial data and calculations remain 100% on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your financial information to our servers or any third parties.

Information We Collect

Personal Financial Data

We Do NOT Collect Your Financial Data All financial information you enter into Finance Planner (including incomes, expenses, savings, account balances, and all calculations) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Website Usage Data

When you visit our Website, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit on our Website
  • Time and date of your visit
  • The website that referred you to our Website

This information is collected by third-party services (described below) and is used only for analytics and advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Website and in our mobile App. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Website and/or other sites on the Internet. This is known as "personalized advertising" or "interest-based advertising."

How to Opt Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the following links:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) Consumer Choice Page
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) Opt-Out Page

Please note that opting out does not mean you will no longer see ads; it means the ads you see will not be personalized based on your interests.

Google AdMob (Mobile App)

In our mobile applications, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information such as:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, device identifiers)
  • App usage data (which features you use, how long you use the app)
  • Ad interaction data (which ads you view or click)

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Website and third-party services use cookies and similar tracking technologies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us and our partners provide you with a better experience.

Types of Cookies We Use:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly. They enable basic functions like language selection and remember your preferences.

Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used by Google AdSense and other advertising partners to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Website and the ads you see.

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect (through third-party services) is used for:

  • Displaying relevant advertisements to keep the service free
  • Understanding how visitors use our Website to improve user experience
  • Maintaining Website functionality (e.g., language preferences)

Reminder: Your financial data (incomes, expenses, savings, calculations) is never collected, transmitted, or used by us or any third party.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Google AdSense/AdMob: Anonymous usage data and advertising identifiers are shared with Google to display ads.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

Your financial data is stored only on your device, using your device's built-in security features. We do not have access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

For the limited website usage data collected by third-party services, we rely on those services' security measures. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Finance Planner is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

International Users

Finance Planner is available worldwide. If you are accessing our Website or App from outside the European Union or United States, please be aware that information collected by third-party services may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users): If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete personal information collected about you. Since your financial data remains on your device, you have complete control over it. For data collected by third parties (like Google), please refer to their privacy policies and opt-out mechanisms.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Financial Data: You have complete control. You can export, back up, or delete your financial data at any time through the App.
  • Cookies: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Personalized Ads: You can opt out of personalized advertising using the links provided in the "Third-Party Services" section above.
  • Questions: If you have questions about your data or this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Significant changes will be communicated through our Website or App.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us:

  • GitHub Discussions: Join our community
  • Contact Page: Visit our contact page

Third-Party Links

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites (such as app stores, social media, and YouTube). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.
