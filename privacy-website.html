Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect the following information through third-party services:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit on our website
  • Time and date of your visit
  • The website that referred you to us

This information is anonymous and is not linked to your personal identity.

How We Use Information

The limited information collected is used for:

  • Displaying relevant advertisements to keep the service free
  • Understanding how visitors use our website to improve user experience
  • Maintaining website functionality (e.g., language preferences)

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve content and user experience.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your browsing activity across websites.

How to Opt Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)

Cookies

Essential Cookies

Necessary for website functionality. They enable basic functions like language selection and remember your preferences.

Advertising Cookies

Used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads based on your browsing activity.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Your Rights

  • Cookies: Manage cookie preferences through your browser settings
  • Personalized Ads: Opt out using the links in the "Third-Party Services" section above
  • Questions: If you have questions, please contact us
