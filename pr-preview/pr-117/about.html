Our Story

Finance Planner was created by Juliaan Meijboom and Colin Wilmans, two passionate advocates for financial independence and smart financial planning. As members of the F.I.R.E. (Financial Independence, Retire Early) movement, they understood firsthand the challenges of planning for long-term financial goals.

Frustrated by expensive financial advisors and complex planning tools that were either too costly or too simplistic, they decided to create a comprehensive, free solution that anyone could use. The result is Finance Planner‚Äîa powerful tool that combines professional-grade financial modeling with an intuitive, user-friendly interface.

Today, Finance Planner helps thousands of people worldwide plan their path to financial independence, model different scenarios, and make informed decisions about their financial future.

What Makes Us Different

  • Comprehensive Planning: Model incomes, expenses, savings, investments, and withdrawals with detailed year-by-year projections.
  • Built for F.I.R.E.: Specifically designed for people pursuing Financial Independence and Early Retirement.
  • Multiple Scenarios: Test different "what-if" scenarios to find the best path to your goals.
  • Inflation-Adjusted: All calculations account for inflation to give you realistic projections.
  • Tax Modeling: Model tax benefits on deposits and tax implications on withdrawals.
  • Mobile Apps: Available on iOS and Android for planning on the go.
