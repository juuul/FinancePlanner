Tax-Advantaged Accounts

Finance Planner helps you model the tax benefits of deposits to retirement accounts:

  • Model tax-advantaged accounts like 401(k), IRA, and other retirement accounts
  • Calculate tax benefits based on your marginal tax rate (e.g., 22% or 24%)
  • See how tax refunds impact your actual savings rate and reduce the real cost of saving
