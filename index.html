Ready to take control of your financial future and achieve Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE)?

Finance Planner is your essential companion for comprehensive, long-term financial planning. It's specifically designed to help you visualize, track, and achieve your FIRE goals. Whether you're just starting your FIRE journey or you're deep into advanced planning, Finance Planner provides the powerful tools and clear insights you need to make informed decisions for your future.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive Multi-Person & Multi-Income Support: Plan for your entire household! Easily manage finances for multiple individuals, various income streams, and diverse expenditure categories.
  • Flexible Savings Accounts: Track and organize multiple savings accounts, allowing you to effectively manage funds allocated for different goals.
  • Long-Term Financial Projections: See your wealth grow over decades with detailed, year-by-year projections. Understand the impact of your savings, investments, and spending on your FIRE timeline.
  • FIRE Goal Tracking: Set your desired FIRE number and track your progress towards financial independence. Our app helps you clearly see how far you've come and how much further you need to go.
  • "What If" Scenarios: Experiment with different savings rates, investment returns, and withdrawal strategies. Instantly see how small changes today can lead to massive differences in your future.
  • Inflation Adjustment: All calculations account for inflation, giving you a realistic picture of your future purchasing power.
  • Detailed Yearly Overviews: Get a clear, actionable summary of your financial position year after year, including net worth, passive income, and projected expenses in retirement.
  • Customizable Inputs: Tailor the app to your unique financial situation. Easily input your income, expenses, investments, assets, and liabilities.
  • Visual Data Representation: Understand complex financial data at a glance with intuitive charts and graphs that clearly illustrate your progress and projections.
  • Retirement Withdrawal Strategies (e.g., 4% Rule): Model different safe withdrawal rates to ensure the longevity of your retirement portfolio.
  • Early Retirement Planning: Specifically designed for the unique challenges and opportunities of early retirement, helping you navigate the accumulation and decumulation phases effectively.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean, intuitive, and easy to navigate, making complex financial planning accessible to everyone.

Why Finance Planner?

  • Clarity for FIRE: We focus on the long-term vision crucial for the FIRE movement, providing a clear roadmap to financial freedom.
  • Empowering Decisions: Gain the knowledge and confidence to make smart financial choices today that will profoundly impact your tomorrow.
  • Beyond Basic Budgeting: While budgeting is important, Finance Planner goes deeper, focusing on the powerful effects of compounding and long-term financial strategy.
  • Stay Motivated: Visualize your progress and stay motivated on your journey to financial independence.

Who is Finance Planner For?

  • Individuals and couples pursuing F.I.R.E. (Financial Independence, Retire Early).
  • Anyone looking for a powerful tool to plan their long-term financial future.
  • Investors seeking to project their wealth growth and retirement readiness.
  • Those who want to understand the long-term impact of their financial decisions.
