Finance Planner: The F.I.R.E Tool

Take Control of Your Financial Future

🚀 NEW APP ALERT! Finance Planner is a brand-new, innovative tool for comprehensive, long-term financial planning. Designed for the F.I.R.E. (Financial Independence, Retire Early) movement, it helps you visualize, track, and achieve your financial goals—whether you’re just starting out or deep into advanced planning.

📈 Actively Developed: We're constantly adding new features and improvements to make your financial planning experience even better!

Current Capabilities

Our new app now supports:

  • Unlimited People & Topics: Add infinite people with infinite income, expense, and savings topics
  • Time-Based Planning: All topics have their own time periods when they are active
  • Smart Calculations: Monthly calculations that take inflation into account for accurate long-term projections

Advanced Features

  • Multi-Person & Multi-Income Support: Manage finances for your entire household, including multiple people, income streams, and spending categories.
  • Flexible Savings Accounts: Organize and track multiple savings accounts for different goals.
  • Long-Term Projections: See your wealth grow over decades with detailed, year-by-year forecasts.
  • FIRE Goal Tracking: Set your FIRE number and monitor your progress toward financial independence.
  • "What If" Scenarios: Experiment with savings rates, investment returns, and withdrawal strategies to see their impact.
  • Inflation Adjustment: All calculations account for inflation, giving you a realistic view of your future purchasing power.
  • Yearly Overviews: Get clear, actionable summaries of your net worth, passive income, and projected retirement expenses.
  • Customizable Inputs: Easily enter your income, expenses, investments, assets, and liabilities.
  • Visual Insights: Intuitive charts and graphs make complex financial data easy to understand.
  • Retirement Withdrawal Strategies: Model safe withdrawal rates (like the 4% rule) to ensure your portfolio lasts.
  • Early Retirement Planning: Navigate both the accumulation and decumulation phases of early retirement.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean, intuitive, and accessible for everyone.

Why Choose Finance Planner?

  • Clarity for FIRE: Focus on the long-term vision crucial for financial independence.
  • Empowering Decisions: Make smart financial choices today for a better tomorrow.
  • Beyond Budgeting: Go deeper than basic budgeting—see the power of compounding and long-term strategy.
  • Stay Motivated: Visualize your progress and stay inspired on your journey.
